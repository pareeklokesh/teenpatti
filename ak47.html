Teen Patti Variation

'Joker' Variation

  • 3 cards will be distributed to all players from a well shuffled deck.
  • The dealer pulls out one card at random after dealing cards to players and nominates all other card of same rank/strength/number as jokers (wild card).
    Example: – If wild card is - A♠, then jokers in the game will be A♣, A♦ & A♥.
  • Betting rules remain same as of simple Teen Patti game.
  • Example: - After dealing cards to players, Dealer pulls & nominates “8♣” as joker. Let’s assume, we get A♥-K♥-8♦. Since any card of “8” will act as joker. Therefore, Final hand with us will be - A♥-K♥-Q♥ (Joker)

'AK47' Variation

The name is derived from the famous weapon “AK47 rifle”.

  • In this 3-patti variation - A(♠♥♦♣), K(♠♥♦♣), 4(♠♥♦♣), 7(♠♥♦♣) of all suits are jokers or wild card.
  • A player with strongest 3 Patti hand will win the game. All other rules and betting remains the same.
  • Example: – let’s assume we get A♠-K♦-4♠, This will become A-A- A (as all three cards Ace, King, 4 are jokers)
  • Example: – Let’s assume we get A♠-2♠-2♥ & opponent has Q♥-4♥-7♣, then the winner will be opponent. (Q-4- 7 = Q-Q- Q as 4 & 7 are joker, while we have 2-2- 2)

'Muflis' Variation

Having a bad day and you are getting worst cards, then this game is for you.

  • In “Muflis” player with the weakest 3-Patti hand will win the game.
  • All rules are like normal teen patti game except a person with weakest hand is a winner.
    • Example, you have K♣-2♦-3♣ while another person has K♥-K♦-9♣, then you are the winner.
    • Example, you have A♥-A♣-A♦ while another person has K♥-K♦-K♠, then other person will win.

'Hukum' Variation

In this variation, you will be dealt with two usual cards and a joker

  • In Hukum Teen Patti, all players are dealt two normal cards and one joker card.
  • The joker card will substitute for any card necessary in order to make a hand.
  • If there are no possible hand combinations, even with the joker card, the card will count as a High Card.

'Lowest Joker' Variation

The lowest card will become JOKER.

  • In this 3 Patti Variation, each player’s lowest ranking card in his/her hand will become Joker.
  • If lowest card is in pair, then that pair is considered as two Jokers.
  • Betting rules will be like normal Teen-Patti game.
  • Example: – let’s assume A♠-K♥-K♣, here lowest cards are both the kings (K♥-K♣) which will act as Joker.
